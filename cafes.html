Beyond Cafe

Beyond Cafe is a cafe located in the heart of Nong Khai city. The cafe offers a good view and easy parking. Their smoothies, coffee, and cakes are all a must-try.

The recommended menu here is toast bread and bingsu, such as strawberry bingsu, mango bingsu, honey toast bread, and various smoothie menus.

Address: Nai Mueang, Mueang Nong Khai District, Nong Khai 43000

Contact @ 095 221 2465

Natit Cafe

Natit Cafe is a coffee shop next to the rice field with a great atmosphere. Everything in this shop is delicious, whether it's coffee or cakes.

Address: Kuan Wan, Mueang Nong Khai District, Nong Khai 43000
