Hotels

Find comfortable and relaxing places to stay in Nong Khai

Amanta Hotel

Amanta Hotel is a top hotel in Nong Khai. It is located next to the Mekong River in the city. There is also a restaurant on the rooftop of the hotel. From the hotel's rooftop, you can have a clear view of the city and the Mekong River. The rooms are well-maintained and worth the price.

Room Rates: Superior Twin Room with Balcony / ฿1165 per night; King bed suite / ฿2500 per night

Asawann Hotel

Asawann Hotel is a hotel located next to Asawann Shopping Mall. You can shop and dine conveniently when staying in this very affordable hotel.

Room Rates: Start at ฿724 per night

Panlaan Boutique Resort Hotel

Panlaan Boutique Resort Hotel, Nong Khai is both a hotel and a resort with a nice view, clean and well-maintained rooms, and a swimming pool.

Room Rates: Start at ฿1000 per night

The Maceo Hotel

The Maceo Hotel is a luxury hotel in the heart of the city, located on the central road or economic road of Nong Khai. When you walk down, you will find many roadside restaurants and there is also a restaurant in the hotel.

Estimated Room Rates: ฿2900 for a room for 2 persons

Royal Nakhara Hotel and Convention Center

This property features a restaurant, free on-site parking and free Wi-Fi. Rooms at Royal Nakhara Hotel are air-conditioned and feature a work desk, minibar, flat-screen satellite TV and an en suite bathroom with toiletries and a hairdryer. The on-site restaurant serves Thai and international cuisine.

Room Rates: Start at ฿1020 per night

Crystal Nong Khai Hotel

This property features a restaurant, free on-site parking and free Wi-Fi. Rooms at Royal Nakhara Hotel are air-conditioned and feature a work desk, minibar, flat-screen satellite TV and an en suite bathroom with toiletries and a hairdryer. The on-site restaurant serves Thai and international cuisine.

Room Rates: Start at ฿799 per night

Silsopa Hotel

Silsopa Hostel is both a guest house and a restaurant. The guest house has a shared bathroom. It is located in the city area, near the market and has many facilities.

Room Rates: Start at ฿589 per night

Bualinn Resort and Hotel

Bualinn Resort and Hotel is a hotel decorated in a natural style, giving a sense of privacy and also has a small swimming pool. The hotel is a bit far from the city center but ride-hailing applications are always available. The accommodation is clean and worth the price.

Room Rates: King Room with Pool View / ฿1050 per night; Superior Twin Room / ฿1090 per night
